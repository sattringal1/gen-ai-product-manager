How It Works

A seamless journey from early idea to Jira-ready execution

1

Share Your Idea

Describe your product idea, strategic question, or initiative in natural language — no templates required.

2

AI Routes & Analyzes

The Orchestrator identifies intent and delegates to the right specialist agent for deep analysis.

3

Get Structured Artifacts

Receive vision statements, lean canvases, OKRs, roadmaps so that they are ready for stakeholder review.

4

Execute in Jira

Export Jira-ready user stories, epics, and acceptance criteria directly into your delivery workflow.

About This Project

The Vision

This system was born from a clear insight: Product Managers and Program Leaders often spend weeks translating early ideas into actionable strategy artifacts. We envision an AI copilot that accelerates this journey — from concept to execution — without sacrificing rigor or strategic depth.

The Approach

Rather than one monolithic AI, this system uses specialized agents — each trained on proven product management frameworks. An intelligent orchestrator understands intent and delegates to the right expert, just like a high-performing product organization.
